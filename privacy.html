Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Beat & Blade uses data when you play through Reddit.

Last updated · July 13, 2026

Data used by the game

Beat & Blade may process and store:

  • Your public Reddit username.
  • Game scores, accuracy, combo-related run data, difficulty, personal bests, rank, and play streak.
  • Your weekly track vote, if you vote.
  • A short proclamation you voluntarily submit while you are the weekly champion.
  • Technical app data needed to run weekly challenges, prevent duplicate scheduled posts, and maintain leaderboards.

The app does not ask for your email address, real name, payment details, precise location, contacts, or private Reddit messages. It does not use third-party advertising or analytics trackers.

Why the data is used

The data is used only to operate the game: save personal bests, calculate rankings and streaks, display leaderboards, create the weekly champion opponent, record votes, publish weekly recaps, and show an optional champion proclamation.

Public information

Your Reddit username and score may appear publicly in game leaderboards and weekly recap posts. A champion proclamation is public when submitted. Do not include personal information in a proclamation.

Storage and sharing

Game data is stored using Reddit Devvit's data services and is processed within Reddit's developer platform. Beat & Blade does not sell personal data. Data is shared only through the public game features described above or when required by law or platform policy.

Retention

Leaderboard, personal-best, streak, voting, and weekly challenge records may be retained while the game operates so that competitive features remain functional. Scheduled-post bookkeeping may also be retained to prevent duplicate posts.

Your choices and requests

You can stop further gameplay data from being created by not using the game. To ask about data associated with your Reddit username, or to request deletion where technically and legally possible, send a Reddit message to u/Background_Depth_807. Identity may need to be confirmed through the same Reddit account.

Children

Beat & Blade is available only to users who are eligible to use Reddit under Reddit's own terms and age requirements.

Changes

This policy may be updated when the game's features or data practices change. The date at the top identifies the latest revision.

Contact

Privacy questions can be sent by Reddit message to u/Background_Depth_807.
